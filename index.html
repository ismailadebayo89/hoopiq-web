How it works

Four steps.
One session.

HoopIQ closes the loop other apps leave open — it doesn't just show you what's wrong, it assigns a drill and tracks whether you improved before you leave the gym.

01

Record your shot

5-second video from the side. Place your phone 10–15 feet away at waist height so your full body is visible.

02

AI analyses your form

MoveNet pose detection maps 17 body joints and measures your elbow angle, release arc, and follow-through in real time.

03

Get a drill instantly

A specific drill is assigned based on your biggest deviation from ideal form. No generic advice — just the right fix.

04

Track your progress

Your form score is tracked across sessions so you can see real improvement over time, not just guesswork.

What we measure

Three metrics.
Measurable results.

Every shot is scored against biomechanically ideal targets with clear tolerances — no vague feedback.

90°
Elbow Angle
The elbow should be directly under the ball at the set point for maximum accuracy.
±15° tolerance
52°
Release Arc
The optimal release angle for the highest chance of the ball going through the net.
±8° tolerance
170°
Follow-Through
Full wrist extension after release — the finishing position that locks in consistency.
±20° tolerance
